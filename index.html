FAQs
How does SOS work?
›
Hold the SOS button for 3 seconds. SAKHI will instantly share your live location with all guardians and automatically call Police (100) and Ambulance (108).
How do I add a guardian?
›
Go to Guardians from the menu. Tap "Add New Guardian" and enter their name and phone number. They'll receive an invite SMS.
Is my location always shared?
›
No. Location is only shared when you tap "Share Location" or trigger SOS. You control this fully from Privacy settings.
What is Fake Call?
›
Fake Call simulates an incoming call on your phone, giving you a reason to leave an uncomfortable situation immediately.
